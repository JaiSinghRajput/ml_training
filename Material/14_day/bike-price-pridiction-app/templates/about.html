About BikePredictor

BikePredictor is a simple, AI-powered tool built to help you know the real worth of your old bike. Whether you're planning to sell, exchange, or just curious — we give you a quick estimate based on real-world data.

Our prediction model uses historical pricing trends, market behavior, and key inputs like kilometers driven, year of purchase, and bike brand/model to give you a near-accurate price.

No login. No nonsense. Just pure prediction power in a beautiful UI ✨
